You can play the game as it exists so for at: http://web.engr.oregonstate.edu/~gipsonb/CS419/aggregate_version_0a/AG-index.html

Click the gold T at the bottom to place a tower. The towers can only be placed along the path and will shoot whenever an 
enemy block is within range. Right click while placing a tower to cancel the
action. Click on an individual tower to see its current stats
